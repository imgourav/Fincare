Request permanent deletion of your FinCare account and associated personal data

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your FinCare account and associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the FinCare app on your Android device and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings → Tap your profile or account icon → Select Account Settings.
  3. Tap "Delete Account" and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your request.
  4. Alternatively, you can email us directly to request deletion (see below).
Request deletion by email If you cannot access the app, email us at:
privacy@fincare.app
Include your registered email address so we can identify your account.

What Data We Delete

When you request account deletion, FinCare will permanently delete the following data associated with your account:

Data Type Status
Account credentials (email, password) Deleted
Profile information (name) Deleted
Budget and savings data Deleted
App preferences and settings Deleted

What to Expect

  • Deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days.
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.
  • If you have an active subscription, you may need to cancel it before deletion. Contact support if you need help.
